# Bio & Personal Background

Shreyas Venkatesha Udupa is a Data Analyst and Business Intelligence Engineer with 2+ years of experience building analytics systems across finance, supply chain, and operations.

He grew up in India and completed his Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering from Vivekanand Education Society's Institute of Technology, Mumbai (University of Mumbai), GPA 3.63, 2018–2022.

He moved to the US for his Master of Science in Information Management (MSIM) at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), GPA 4.0, August 2023 – May 2025. He was inducted into the Phi Kappa Phi Honor Society — top 10% of his graduating class.

He is currently based in Champaign, IL.

## Origin Story
Shreyas got into data watching Formula 1 races in high school. Seeing thousands of sensors send real-time telemetry data that drove split-second pit stop decisions and predictive overtake models made him realise data was the difference between winning and losing. He taught himself Python, SQL, and Tableau as his foundation.
